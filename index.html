About Me

Abhi Kasanagottu
Project Analyst/Business Administration Support

As a Project Analyst and Admin, I am a strong leader and understand how to proactively coordinate projects and support other peoples' lives!

Starting my career at Fannie Mae allowed me to learn a plethora of skills. Early in my 2.5 years, I was tasked with critical deliverables that impacted divisions such as the entirety of Single-Family. Additionally, I was Single-Family's Executive Vice President's and Vice President's admin, essentially managing the day-to-day lives of two fierce and well known leaders.

Fannie Mae (FNMA) is a GSE that provides liquidity, affordability, and stability in the mortgage industry to facilitate successful homeownership. While practicing lean management and absorbing many valuable lessons, I have also learned many project management skills. I currently coordinate the Single-Family Year Up Internship as well as other internship programs at FNMA. Alongside this program, I support the Appraiser Diversity Initiative Program at Fannie Mae which falls under our company's Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion efforts. On top of these responsibilities, I assist with special projects such as my company's Recognition efforts through our celebratory focus pieces on hardworking individuals, internal awards platform, and external awards. I am skilled at owning day-to-day engagements and deliverables and am constantly eager to learn and develop professionally.

I studied Psychology/Biology at Virginia Commonwealth University (2016-2019).
